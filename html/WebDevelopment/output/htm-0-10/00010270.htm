Information / Notification

  • Sarathi and Vahan are the two flagship applications of MoRTH. These applications are brought under Mission Mode Project by NeGP which are Driven by Ministry of Road Transport & Highways (MoRTH).

  • Sarathi & Vahan are designed, developed, implemented and supported by NIC. These applications are implemented across 1000+ RTOs of the country. The Driving Licence data is consolidated at State and National level through periodic replication process.

  • Sarathi-on-Web facilitates public to submit applications for LL & DL related transactions thru this mode. The application data is in turn transferred to the remote RTO/DTOs of the states for further processing of the request (submitted thru ONLINE) at the local RTO/DTO Office.

  • Services like E-payment GW, CSC , SSDG and Service partners are also being provided.
