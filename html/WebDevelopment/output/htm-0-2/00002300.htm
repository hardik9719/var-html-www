\  Adjusts the preview scale: 
\  press <plus> to scale in 
\  press <minus> to scale out 
\  press Ctrl-<plus> to Zoom In 
\  press Ctrl-<minus> to Zoom Out 
\  press /  to set 100% 
\  press * to customize view 
\  mouse mouse wheel with Ctrl to Zoom In/Out 
\  mouse mouse wheel with right button to Zoom In/Out 
\  double click by left mouse button to Zoom In 
\  double click by right mouse button to Zoom Out
